DIONWIRED

Dion Wired is one of South Africa's premier consumer electrical & home appliances concept stores. Operating in over 24 stores throughout South Africa, Dion Wired aims to give customers the optimal shopping experience at the most affordable prices.

In addition, Dion Wired is a subsidiary of the JSE listed Massmart Holdings, the third largest distributor of consumer goods and also the leading retailer of general merchandise, liquor, home improvement equipment and supplies, and the leading wholesaler of basic foods in Africa.

Dion Wired stores are also easy to locate with branches in Menlyn, Gateway, Baywest, Pavilion, Cresta, and other popular shopping malls nationwide.

The products offered at Dion Wired include TV and audio equipment, appliances, computers, laptops and computing equipment, mobile and smart technology products, games, consoles and gadgets, fridges, and photography and navigation equipment. They source the best products from around the world and show their customers how they can be used to simplify their lives.

Customers additionally prize Dion Wired as their preferred electronics and appliances store as a result of their great deals and frequent promotions. These regular specials offered by the company ensure that customers get their desired items at the lowest prices on the market.

Specials often include TV specials, appliance specials, laptop specials, and yearly Black Friday sale items.
