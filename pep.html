PEP

PEP is a South African institution that started in 1965 with one store. Today PEP is the largest single brand retailer in the country with stores in almost every town and village, where it often provides a lifeline to local people with affordable, good quality merchandise.

As well as clothing and footwear, PEP sells homeware, FMCG, cellular and airtime products, and also offers a range of other services such as cash-backs, Capfin loans, funeral insurance, cross-border and local money transfers, selected bill payments(such as DSTV), Flash tokens, PAXI parcel service and more.
